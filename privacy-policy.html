Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Spectrum Custom Interiors ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the spectrumOS application ("Application"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name and email address provided through Google Single Sign-On.
  • Business Data: Estimates, project details, line items, client information, and other data you input into the Application.
  • Integration Data: Data accessed through connected third-party services (e.g., QuickBooks Online, Google Workspace) as authorized by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the Application;
  • Create and manage estimates, projects, and related documents;
  • Integrate with third-party services you have authorized;
  • Improve the functionality and user experience of the Application.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or business data with third parties except as necessary to:

  • Operate integrations you have explicitly authorized (e.g., sending estimate data to QuickBooks Online);
  • Comply with legal obligations or enforce our agreements.

4. Third-Party Services

The Application integrates with third-party services including but not limited to Intuit QuickBooks Online, Google Workspace (Docs, Drive, Gmail), and others. When you connect these services, we access only the data necessary to perform the functions you request. Each third-party service has its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review them.

Specifically regarding QuickBooks Online: we access your QuickBooks data solely to create estimates and retrieve PDF documents as directed by you. We do not access, store, or use your QuickBooks financial data for any other purpose.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Access to the Application is restricted to authorized users only through secure authentication.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Application's services. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Request correction or deletion of your data;
  • Disconnect third-party service integrations at any time;
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Spectrum Custom Interiors.
